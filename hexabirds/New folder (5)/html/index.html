Roadmap

Voxel Palace is more than a piece of artwork. It’s the future of investing, and offers a unique method of generating passive income and financial education.

Mint

It’s simple - 5,000 Voxel Palace NFTs will be minted onto the blockchain. Mint price will be 0.1 ETH, with a limit of 5 NFTs per wallet.

NFT Reveal

Less than 24 hours after mint we’ll reveal our NFTs so our holders can see what they've minted. Using a series of X randomly selected traits, each NFT has a place on a rarity scale

Token Launch

We’ll release our $DIME token, the first phase of our planned utility

DAO Access

Holders will be placed into a community-governed DAO, holding fractional power over the 500 ETH-funded wallet.

Flip and Sell

Using the DAO, holders will get to decide on which properties to invest in (with advice from professionals), and have the power to flip or hold onto property.

Relax

Profits from property investment will be shared out to holders using our $DIME token. A percentage of funds will be put back into the DAO to ensure continuous cash flow. Now you get to enjoy the passive income of Voxel Palace, and remain part of the community for as long as you wish.
